Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 May 2026

TapClash ("the app") is a reaction-speed game for the Solana Seeker. We designed it to collect as little as possible. There are no accounts, no email sign-up, no advertising, and no third-party analytics or tracking.

What we collect

To run the global leaderboard, the app submits the following when you choose to post a score:

  • Your Solana wallet public address (a public identifier — never your private key)
  • Your game results for a round: score, hits, misses, accuracy, game mode, round duration
  • A random one-time nonce and your wallet's cryptographic signature of the score, used to verify the submission is genuine

That's it. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, location, device identifiers, or any other personal information.

Your wallet & keys

Wallet authentication and signing happen entirely through the Solana Seed Vault on your device. Your private keys never leave your device and are never transmitted to or stored by us.

Where data is stored

  • Leaderboard: submitted scores are stored on a Cloudflare Workers backend, organized by season and game mode. Best score per wallet per season is retained.
  • Blockchain: if you opt into a paid prize pool (a future feature), the relevant transactions are recorded publicly on the Solana blockchain. On-chain data is public and permanent by nature.
  • On your device: your local stats and last-used settings are stored on-device only.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Leaderboard entries (a shortened wallet address and score) are visible to other players by design — that is the point of a public leaderboard.

Data retention & deletion

Leaderboard data is associated with a season. Because submissions are tied only to a public wallet address you control, you can stop submitting at any time. To request removal of a leaderboard entry, contact us at the email below with the wallet address in question. On-chain transactions cannot be deleted, as they are part of the public Solana ledger.

Children

TapClash is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "last updated" date.
