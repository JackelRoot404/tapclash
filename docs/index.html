TapClash
Tap fast. Stay accurate. Win the season.

TapClash is a 30-second reaction-speed game built natively for the Solana Seeker. Targets pop up — tap them before they vanish. Hit early for bonus points, chain them for combos, and aim for perfect accuracy. Every score is signed by your Seed Vault wallet and posted to a global monthly leaderboard.

Game modes

Classic Frenzy Precision Sudden Death

Each mode has its own independent leaderboard. Dodge the bombs, grab the gold bonus targets, and find the mode that fits your style.

Why TapClash

  • 30-second rounds you can play in line for coffee
  • Four modes + varied targets (bonus, mini, bomb) — each with its own board
  • Monthly seasons reset on the 1st, UTC — a fresh start every month
  • Your wallet, your scores — no email, no account, no tracking
  • Built on the Solana Seed Vault — private keys never leave your device
